# CBOE EDGX Equities Fee Schedule

**Date**: 2025-01-27
**Source**: <https://www.cboe.com/us/equities/membership/fee_schedule/edgx/>
**Exchange**: EDGX Equities (CBOE EDGX Exchange)

---

## Overview

This document outlines the fee structure for CBOE EDGX Equities exchange, including market data feeds, connectivity, and additional services. Understanding these costs is critical for calculating total transaction costs when trading box spreads and other options strategies.

---

## Cboe One Feed (Market Data)

CBOE's consolidated market data feed providing equity and options data.

### Internal Distribution

| Feed Type | Distribution Fee | Professional User | Non-Professional User | Enterprise Fee |
|-----------|------------------|-------------------|----------------------|----------------|
| **Cboe One Summary** | $1,500/month | $10.00/month | $0.25/month | $50,000/month |
| **Cboe One Premium** | $15,000/month | $0/month | $0/month | N/A |

### External Distribution

| Feed Type | Distribution Fee | Professional User | Non-Professional User | Enterprise Fee | Digital Media Enterprise |
|-----------|------------------|-------------------|----------------------|----------------|-------------------------|
| **Cboe One Summary** | $5,000/month¹,⁷ | $10/month | $0.25/month | $50,000/month | $15,000/month |
| **Cboe One Premium** | $12,500/month¹,⁷ | $15/month | $0.50/month | $100,000/month | $25,000/month |

#### Notes

1. **New External Distributor Credit**:
   - Cboe One Summary: No External Distributor Fee for first 1 month
   - Cboe One Premium: No External Distributor Fee for first 3 months

2. **User Fee Credit**: External Distributors eligible for credit against monthly Distributor Fee equal to User Fees (up to maximum of External Distributor Fee)

3. **Enterprise License**: Alternative to per-user fees, covers unlimited Professional and Non-Professional Users. Separate Enterprise Fee required for each Distributor.

4. **Data Consolidation Fee**: $1,000/month

5. **Digital Media Enterprise**: For television, websites, and mobile devices (informational/non-trading purposes only)

6. **Small Retail Broker Distribution Program**:
   - Distribution Fee: $3,500/month (vs. $5,000/month)
   - Data Consolidation Fee: $350/month (vs. $1,000/month)
   - Eligibility:
     - Distributor is broker-dealer with brokerage relationship
     - ≥90% of subscribers are Non-Professional
     - ≤10,000 Non-Professional Data Users

7. **New Uncontrolled External Distributor Fee Waiver**:
   - No External Distribution Fee or Data Consolidation Fee until first User is enlisted
   - Applies separately to Summary and Premium feeds
   - Eligibility: Must not have received applicable feed(s) within last 18 months

8. **Small Retail Broker Hosted Solution Program**:
   - $3,500/month Distribution Fee waived once first External Hosted Subscriber is subscribed
   - External Hosted Subscriber: $850/month fixed cost (vs. $0.25/user/month)
   - Pro User costs billed separately
   - Eligibility: Same as Small Retail Broker Distribution Program
   - Ineligibility: >10,000 Non-Professional Data Users

## Edge Attribution Incentive Program

**Purpose**: Incentive program for members providing liquidity through Attributable Orders.

### Eligibility Requirements

- Enter Attributable Orders into Exchange System
- At least **100 symbols** over **10 consecutive trading days** within a month

### Incentive Structure

- **Revenue Allotment**: 25% of revenue from EdgeBook Attributed fees
- **Payment Basis**: Percentage of executed share volume from Attributable Orders
- **Monthly Qualification**: Must meet eligibility requirements each month

### Relevance to Box Spread Trading

- Provides rebate opportunity for market makers and liquidity providers
- May offset transaction costs for high-volume strategies
- Requires consistent quoting across multiple symbols

## US Equity Short Volume & Trades Report

End-of-day report providing short volume and trade data for US equities.

### Pricing

| Delivery Type | Fee |
|---------------|-----|
| Monthly Fee per Internal Distributor | $750 |
| Monthly Fee per External Distributor | $1,250 |
| Fee per Month of Historical Data²,³ | $500 |

### Notes

1. **Subscription Options**: Monthly or annual basis
   - Monthly: Access from subscription date, end-of-month report on last business day
   - Annual: Includes 12 months of historical data at no extra charge

2. **Historical Data Discount** (July 29, 2025 - September 30, 2025):
   - 20% discount on single purchases totaling $20,000 or more
   - Cannot be combined with other discounts

3. **Academic Discount**:
   - Qualifying Academic Purchasers: 50% discount OR $500 minimum (whichever is greater)
   - Example: $750 purchase → $375 discount → charged $500 (greater of $375 or $500)

## Cboe Timestamping Service

Service providing detailed order entry and cancellation reports for latency analysis and compliance.

### Missed Liquidity Report

| Number of Order Entry Ports (FIX or BOE) | Fee |
|-------------------------------------------|-----|
| 1-10 Ports | $1,500/month |
| 11-20 Ports | $2,000/month |
| ≥21 Ports | $2,500/month |

**Note**: Fee is not progressive (e.g., 20 ports = $2,000/month total, not $1,500 + $2,000)

### Missed Cancels Report

| Per Member or Sponsored Participant | $1,000/month |

### All Cancels Report

| Per Member or Sponsored Participant | $1,000/month |

### Missed Cancels + All Cancels Report (Bundle)

| Per Member or Sponsored Participant | $1,500/month |

### Mid-Month Subscriptions

- Initial month prorated based on subscription date
- Missed Liquidity Report tier based on maximum number of subscribing Ports during month
- Tier changes not prorated

### Access

- Either Member or Sponsored Participant may subscribe directly
- Separate subscriptions for each report type

## Cost Analysis for Box Spread Trading

### Typical Setup Costs

#### Minimal Setup (Retail/Individual)

- **Market Data**: Cboe One Summary (Non-Professional): $0.25/month/user
- **Connectivity**: Free BZX/BYX/EDGX/EDGA feeds
- **Order Entry**: Free for CBOE exchanges
- **Total**: ~$0.25/month (plus broker fees)

#### Professional Setup

- **Market Data**: Cboe One Premium (Internal): $15,000/month
- **Options Data**: OPRA + SIP Bundle: $6,390/month
- **Order Entry**: 10Mb Unicast: $1,250/month
- **Total**: ~$22,640/month (plus exchange transaction fees)

#### Enterprise Setup

- **Market Data**: Enterprise license: $50,000-$100,000/month
- **Full Data Suite**: $20,000-$30,000/month
- **High-Speed Connectivity**: 100Mb: $3,500/month
- **Total**: ~$73,500-$133,500/month

### Cost Optimization Strategies

1. **Use Free Feeds**: Leverage free BZX/BYX/EDGX/EDGA feeds when possible
2. **Bundle Discounts**: Use SIP bundles for 10-40% savings
3. **Small Retail Program**: Qualify for reduced fees if ≥90% Non-Professional users
4. **Enterprise License**: Cost-effective for >5,000 users
5. **Volume-Based Rebates**: Combine with Frequent Trader Program (FTID) for fee rebates

### Transaction Cost Impact

For box spread strategies:

- **Market Data**: Fixed monthly cost (amortized per trade)
- **Exchange Fees**: Per-contract fees (reduced by FTID rebates)
- **Connectivity**: Fixed monthly cost
- **Total Cost**: Market data + connectivity + (exchange fees - rebates)

**Example**:

- 10,000 SPX contracts/month
- SPX transaction fee: $0.45/contract = $4,500
- FTID Tier 1 rebate (3%): $135 rebate
- Net transaction cost: $4,365
- Plus market data/connectivity: $22,640/month
- **Total**: ~$27,005/month

## Integration Considerations

### For Automated Trading Systems

1. **Market Data Requirements**:
   - OPRA feed essential for options pricing
   - Cboe One Premium for comprehensive CBOE data
   - SIP bundle for equity market data

2. **Connectivity**:
   - Unicast Access for low-latency order entry
   - Bandwidth sizing based on order volume
   - Free access to CBOE exchanges reduces costs

3. **Cost Tracking**:
   - Monitor monthly data/connectivity costs
   - Track FTID rebates to offset transaction fees
   - Optimize bundle selection for cost efficiency

4. **Compliance**:
   - Ensure proper user classification (Professional vs. Non-Professional)
   - Maintain audit trail for fee calculations
   - Document data usage for reporting

# QuantPedia Subscription - Trading Strategy Research Platform

**Date**: 2025-01-27
**Source**: <https://quantpedia.com/pricing/>

## Overview

QuantPedia is a subscription-based research platform providing access to quantitative trading strategies, academic research papers, backtests, and portfolio analysis tools. The platform offers three subscription tiers (Prime, Premium, Pro) with varying levels of access to strategies, research, and analytical tools.

---

## Subscription Tiers

### 1. QuantPedia Prime

**Target Audience**: Aspirational quants and individual systematic investors

**Features**:

- **✓ Limited Access**: 100+ essential Premium strategies (out of 900+)

- **✕ Quant Reports**: No access to 30+ Quant Reports

## About Enochian Magic

**Enochian Magic** is a system of ceremonial magic developed by John Dee and Edward Kelley in the 16th century. It involves:

- **Angelic language** (Enochian)
- **Calls** (invocations to angels)
- **Aethyrs** (30 spiritual realms)
- **Watchtowers** (four elemental tablets)
- **Angelic entities** and hierarchies

**Key Themes:**

- Communication with divine entities
- Structured systems and hierarchies
- Knowledge and revelation
- Protection and guidance

### 10. **Complex Enochian Names** ⚠️

**Enochian System**: Contains many longer names, but most are:

- ⚠️ Very long and complex (e.g., "MOR-DIAL-HKTGA", "ORO-IBAH-AOZPI")
- ⚠️ Difficult to pronounce
- ⚠️ May have religious/occult connotations

**Examples:**

- **Mordialhktga** - "He who burns up iniquity without equal"
- **Oroibahaozpi** - "He who cries aloud in the place of desolation"
- **Mpharslgaiol** - "He who is the first true creator"

**Recommendation**: ⚠️ Not recommended - too complex for software naming

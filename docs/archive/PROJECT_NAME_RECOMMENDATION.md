## 🏆 Top Recommendation: **Hephaestus**

### Why Hephaestus?

**Perfect Semantic Fit:**

- ✅ **God of automatons** - Literally created mechanical automatons in Greek mythology
- ✅ **Master craftsman** - Builds tools and systems (like our automation tools)
- ✅ **Inventor** - Creates innovative solutions
- ✅ **Fire and forge** - Powers automation (like our MCP server)

**Practical Benefits:**

- ✅ **Unique** - Less common than Athena/Hermes
- ✅ **Memorable** - Strong, distinctive name
- ✅ **Professional** - Works for enterprise tools
- ✅ **Excellent availability** - Few conflicts in software space

**Package Names:**

- npm: `hephaestus-mcp`
- PyPI: `hephaestus-automation-mcp`
- GitHub: `hephaestus-project-automation`
